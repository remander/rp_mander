Experiment Procedure

The outlined steps below give a brief overview of the experiment.
Click the dropdowns if more clarification is needed.


Step

Description

Image

1

Out of the five regulation models of basketballs, choose one to carry out the next steps of the procedure. All basketballs will eventually be selected for testing.

2

To ensure that the basketball are tested under the same conditions, inflate each basketball to the predetermined PSI level.

3

Another constant of the experiment is the height from which the basketball is dropped. By loading the basketball onto the dropping apparatus, this constant height is maintained.

4

Since the basketballs are all loaded onto the same dropping apparatus, they will all be released from the same height and onto the floor.

5

Throughout the testing of a basketball model, its behavior should be recorded and measured. This includes taking a video recording of the basketball's bounce, measuring the height of the bounce and taking its PSI measurement.

6

Whenever a new basketball model is chosen, the steps above should be repeated until the designated number of drops is reached.
